Our Services

Amazing is a luxury interior design company based in Karen Nairobi and Sinza Dar esalaam. We offer a full range of interior and exterior design services to bring your vision to life! From stunning ceilings to elegant outdoor spaces.

They include:
  • Gypsum Ceiling Designs
  • Custom Wall Painting & Finishing
  • Stylish Floor Tiling & Wooden Flooring
  • Tailored Kitchens & Wardrobes
  • Exquisite Wainscoting for an Elegant Touch
  • Professional Plumbing & Fittings
  • Expert Electrical Wiring & Lighting Solutions
